FAQs

Frequently asked about pricing

Everything you need to know about our plans.

Is the free plan really free forever? +
Yes. The free plan is free forever - no credit card, no trial period. You only need to upgrade if you exceed 50 transactions a month or need features like staff commissions or SMS.
Can I switch plans at any time? +
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the end of your billing period.
How do I pay? +
We accept MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, Visa, and Mastercard. Monthly billing is automatic. Annual billing gives you a 20% discount.
What happens if I exceed my transaction limit? +
We'll notify you when you're approaching the limit. Your service won't be interrupted - we'll just ask you to upgrade before the next billing cycle.
